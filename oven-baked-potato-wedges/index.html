Oven-baked Potato Wedges

Want to eat potato wedges but feeling heavy with the fried oil? No worries, we have another simpler and mess-free option - oven baked mashed potato!

Time Required

  • Preparation time - 10 minutes
  • Cooking time - 15-20 minutes

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cut the potato into prism shape. (Cut into similar size if possible to ensure uniform heating)
  2. Pat the potato wedges dry before applying spices.
  3. You may add corn starch at this step.
  4. Coat the potato wedges with oil and spices.
  5. Align the wedges and bake them at 200¬∞C for 20 minutes.

  • Dip with corn starch (and make sure your wedges is dry) to make the wedges crunchier üåü
