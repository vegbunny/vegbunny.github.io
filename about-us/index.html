About Us

Veg Bunny is moderated by an aspiring couple who strive to promote a vegan lifestyle and eco-awareness to more people.

The journey started when both the bunnies watched a life-changing documentary named, "What The Health" in 2020 (Quite late, isn't it?)  Since then, they always try their best to adopt an environmentally friendly lifestyle, including going vegan.

Most of the ideation, production. composition and cooking are done by the 'her' bunny, meanwhile the 'his' bunny serves as the 'food tester', handles the production quality assurance and technology setups.

Thank you for visiting our website, and hope this could be a place to inspire you to explore more about eco-friendly, sustainable lifestyle. Peace!
