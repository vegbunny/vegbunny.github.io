Mashed Potatoes

Jul 24, 2021 1 min read Others

It is no doubt that KFC mashed potatoes are the best, but do you know that you can make them easily at home with simple ingredients? Let's make delicious homemade mashed potatoes to fill up our tummies.

Time Required

  • Preparation time - 5 minutes
  • Cooking time - 15 minutes

Ingredients

The measurements below are based on 4 servings.

You will need:

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes (approx. 100g each)
  • 10g Plant-based butter

Adjust according to your taste preference:

  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Black and white pepper powder
  • Dried basil leaves

The sauce:

  • 1 tbsp Vegetarian oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Corn starch
  • Water

Cooking Instructions

  1. Dice the potato and soak them in salt water for a while.
  2. You can either steam or boil the potatoes.
  3. Prepare the sauce by combining the cornstarch with water to form a cornstarch slurry. Slowly stir until the starch dissolves in the water.
  4. Simmer the cornstarch slurry. Add vegetarian oyster sauce and black pepper powder. Stop heating upon reaching desired thickness.
  5. Add plant-based butter to the steamed potato and mash them. You may use a blender if you prefer the creamier version.
  6. Add dried basil leaves and mix the mashed potatoes.
  7. Enjoy your mashed potato with the warm sauce!
