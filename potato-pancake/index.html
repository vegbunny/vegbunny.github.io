Potato Pancake

Sep 4, 2021 1 min read Potato

Time Required

  • Preparation time - 20 minutes
  • Cooking time - 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 medium-sized potatoes (approx. 100g each)

Adjust according to your taste preference

  • Canola oil (2 tablespoon)
  • Paprika
  • Mixed herbs
  • Black pepper powder
  • Salt

Cooking Instructions

  1. Mix the flax with water and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Stir fry the cabbage and carrot until it is cooked.
  3. In a bowl, add the dry ingredients (all-purpose flour, shredded potatoes, stir-fried cabbage and carrot, corn starch).
  4. While mixing the ingredients, slowly add the water until reaches desired thickness.
  5. Add salt, black pepper powder, white pepper powder into the mixture.
  6. Pan-fry the mixture until both sides turn golden brown.

üí° Tip:

  • Add 1 teaspoon of corn starch if you prefer the crispier version.
  • Flax is an egg replacement by grinding flax seeds and mix with water so that it forms the gooey-like structure to clump
  • Add some corn bits to add some sweetness and thus enhance the overall flavour.
